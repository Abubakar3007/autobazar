2.2 Statistical evaluations and needs-based design (cookies)

When using our offer, we create usage profiles using pseudonyms for the purposes of statistical evaluation and the needs-based design of the offer. This processing is carried out on the basis of legal regulations that allow personal data to be processed because AutoScout24 has an overriding legitimate interest in a needs-based design that is geared to usage habits and the statistical evaluation of our offer (including Section 15 (3) TMG, Art. 6 Para. 1 f) GDPR).

To do this, we use so-called cookies, ie alphanumeric identifiers that we transmit to your computer via your web browser. They allow our systems to temporarily distinguish your browser from others. On the one hand, we will send you so-called persistent cookies, which we use to record the search criteria you enter in our car database and save them for our internal evaluations.

On the other hand, we use so-called session cookies, with the help of which you receive a session ID for the duration of your visit to our website, which enables us to recognize you as a specific (anonymous) user. This allows us to create continuity for you during your visit and, for example, avoid you being repeatedly shown the same advertisements. The session cookie and your session ID are deleted at the end of your visit to our site.

You can set cookies by deactivating cookies in your web browser. The help function in the menu bar of most web browsers explains how to set your browser so that it accepts no cookies or only accepts them to a limited extent. There you will also find out how to configure your browser so that it informs you when a cookie is "offered" to your computer. Unfortunately, you can only fully use and experience the functionality of our website with an activated acceptance of cookies.

We therefore recommend that you leave your browser's cookie function switched on when you visit our website. We also recommend that you always log off completely after you have finished using a computer that you share with others and whose browser is set to accept cookies. If you do not accept cookies, it is unfortunately not possible for us to provide you with all the functions and services of our platform.

Unless you have objected, we use different methods for the above-mentioned purposes of statistical evaluation and the needs-based design of our offer.

Die INFOnline GmbH erhebt die folgenden Daten, welche nach DSGVO einen Personenbezug aufweisen:

Das Messverfahren der INFOnline GmbH, welches auf dieser Webseite eingesetzt wird, ermittelt Nutzungsdaten.
